About Me
Designing Is My Passion

Hi, I’m Jungmin, a graphic designer with roots in contemporary art and a passion for bold visual storytelling. My journey began with a degree in fine art, where I explored abstract forms and conceptual ideas. Today, I’m studying graphic design to merge those artistic foundations with practical, impactful communication. I’m driven by clean design, innovative typography, and the power of color to evoke emotion. Whether I’m crafting a brand identity or designing for print, I aim to create work that resonates and inspires. When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me exploring art galleries, curating playlists, or diving into a new creative project. Let’s create something amazing together!
